Clothing Donation Platform

Problem Statement

Every year, millions of people face challenges accessing basic clothing due to poverty or disasters. While many individuals and organizations are willing to donate, the lack of an efficient platform to manage and track donations results in wastage and unmet needs.

Solution

This platform bridges the gap between donors and recipients by providing a streamlined interface to manage donations. It ensures transparency, tracks donations, and facilitates timely distribution to those in need.
